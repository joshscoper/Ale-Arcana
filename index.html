Welcome to Ale & Arcana

Embark on an epic journey in Ale & Arcana, where your humble tavern is the heart of a sprawling, medieval realm. Gather rare resources, master new recipes, and upgrade your bustling inn to attract daring adventurers. Uncover hidden lore, complete heroic quests, and shape your own legacy in this immersive, RPG-inspired world.

Tavern Management

Build and expand your tavern to unlock new recipes, recruit staff, and serve adventurers.

Epic RPG Adventure

Embark on quests, battle creatures, and level up your character in a rich fantasy world.

Resource Gathering

Mine, fish, farm, and collect resources to upgrade buildings and craft powerful items.

Co-op Play

Play solo or join up to three friends in a party to take on dungeons and challenges together.

Story & Lore

Unravel the history of Ale & Arcana through interactive lore, NPC dialogues, and hidden secrets.

Dungeons & Raids

Face dangerous enemies, solve puzzles, and claim legendary loot in dungeon instances.
